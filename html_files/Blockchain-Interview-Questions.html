  • Blockchain Interview Questions
  1. What is blockchain technology, and how does it work?
  2. Explain the concept of decentralization in blockchain.
  3. What is a smart contract, and how does it function in blockchain?
  4. Describe the process of consensus in blockchain and provide examples of consensus algorithms.
  5. What are the advantages and limitations of blockchain technology?
  6. Discuss the difference between public and private blockchains.
  7. What is a fork in blockchain, and what are the different types of forks?
  8. Explain the concept of immutability in blockchain and how it ensures data integrity.
  9. How does blockchain ensure security and privacy?
  10. Discuss real-world use cases of blockchain technology in various industries.

These questions should give you a good starting point for preparing for a blockchain interview. It's important to have a solid understanding of blockchain concepts, consensus algorithms, smart contracts, and the practical applications of blockchain technology. Keeping up with the latest trends and developments in the field will also be beneficial. Good luck!
